--- layout: post title: How to download older versions of the Android SDK ---

The main root url is:

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/

Depending on the specific version you want to download, you just append to the above path the right file. It may take some guesswork, but the following are known to exist:

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r16-macosx.zip

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r17-macosx.zip

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r18-macosx.zip

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r20.0.3-macosx.zip



https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r16-linux.tgz

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r17-linux.tgz

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r18-linux.tgz

https://dl-ssl.google.com/android/android-sdk_r20.0.3-linux.tgz

Here’s where to download the specific platform-tools binary:

http://dl-ssl.google.com/android/repository/platform-tools_r11-macosx.zip

http://dl-ssl.google.com/android/repository/platform-tools_r12-macosx.zip
